Duncan’s Snake

Click “Start game!” and use the buttons that appear (or WASD keys) to change your snake’s direction.

The snake grows when its head (dark green) collides with the food, and the food moves to a new position.

Your snake can go through the edge of the board and appear on the other side.

It’s game over if the snake bites itself.

Your snake has bitten itself!

Game over. Your snake was 5 joints long.

Length of snake: 2
